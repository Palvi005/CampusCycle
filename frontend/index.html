How it works

One item. Many students. Zero waste.

Step 01
📋
List or Request

Post what you have or what you need. Books, calculators, lab coats — anything goes.

Step 02
⚖️
Fair Allocation

Our reuse score system gives priority to students who contribute most to the community.

Step 03
📦
Receive & Reuse

Collect the item from a verified student. Use it for as long as you need it.

Step 04
🔁
Pass it Forward

Done? Pass the item to the next student. Earn score. Keep the cycle going.

Key innovation

Built for campuses. Designed for reuse.

🔒
Verified Students Only

Sign up with your .edu email. Every user is verified. No outsiders, no spam — just your campus community.

⭐
Smart Reuse Scoring

Your activity earns you a reuse score. Higher score = priority access to high-demand items. Contribute more, get more.

📱
QR Handoff System

Confirm item transfers securely with a two-way QR scan. Instant verification, no trust issues.

🔄
Continuous Reuse

Unlike buy/sell, items stay in the cycle. One calculator can serve 10 students across 5 semesters.

💬
In-App Messaging

Coordinate handoffs without sharing personal contacts. Private, campus-scoped messaging built-in.

🌍
Campus Ecosystem

A self-sustaining loop of giving and taking. The more students join, the more powerful it gets.

Reuse Score

Every time you share an item, pass it forward, or help a fellow student — your Reuse Score grows. It's your campus reputation, your contribution, your badge of honour.
